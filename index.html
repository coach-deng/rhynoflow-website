FAQ

Questions I always get.

What do I need to get started?

A Google Workspace account (Gmail, Calendar, Drive). That's it. If you don't have one, I'll help you set it up.

Do I need to be technical?

Not even a little bit. I set everything up. You just check your email like you normally do, except now half the work is already done for you.

What if I want to cancel?

Then you cancel. No contracts, no hard feelings. Everything I built stays in your Google account. It's yours.

How fast can I get started?

Solo is live in about a week. Business takes around two weeks. Either way, you'll see the first things working within days. Not weeks of waiting around.

Can I start small and add more later?

Yeah. Most people start with Solo and add more once they see what they're getting back. I just build on top of what's already there.

What if something breaks?

That's what the monthly fee covers. I keep an eye on everything. If Google updates something or a script hiccups, I fix it. You don't have to worry about it.

Is my data safe?

Your data never leaves your Google account. I don't store it, I don't see it after setup. The scripts run inside your own workspace, same security as the rest of your Google stuff.

Do you work with foreninger and associations?

That's where this started. I built it for my own sports club. Foreninger and volunteer orgs are honestly the best use case because admin is brutal when nobody's getting paid to do it. There's a Forening plan in the pricing section made for exactly that.
