FAQ

Common questions.

What kind of projects do you take on?

Honestly, a lot of things. Automations, web apps, dashboards, APIs, landing pages, AI tools. The common thread is that it solves a real problem for your business. We know Google Workspace and React really well but we're comfortable across the full stack. If you're not sure whether we can help, just ask.

How fast can you deliver?

Faster than you'd expect. A simple automation or landing page can be live in a day or two. A full web app usually takes about a week. We scope it together on the first call so there are no surprises on either side.

Do I need to be technical?

No. You describe the problem: what's taking too long, what's breaking, what you wish existed. We handle the rest and explain it in plain language. No jargon, no ticket systems, no waiting for someone to respond.

What does getting started look like?

We jump on a 30-minute call. You walk us through what you need. We give you a clear scope and price, and if it makes sense, we start. Most clients are live within a week of that first conversation.

How is this different from hiring an agency?

At an agency you get account managers, project managers, and a junior developer who's never spoken to you. Weeks go by and nothing ships. Here you talk directly to the person building your project. That's it. Faster decisions, faster delivery, and you always know what's happening.

Who owns the code?

You do, fully. The source code, your data, your hosting. If we ever stop working together, everything keeps running exactly as it did. Google Apps Script automations live inside your own Google account. Web apps sit on your infrastructure. We don't hold anything over your head.

What if something breaks?

Monthly clients get continuous support: we catch most things before you even notice. For one-off projects, 30 days of support is included. After that, we have affordable retainers if you want someone keeping an eye on things long-term.

What if I want to cancel?

You just cancel. No contracts, no exit fees, no awkward conversations. Everything you've built stays yours and keeps working.

Let's talk about what you need built.

30 minutes, no pitch. You tell us the problem, we tell you what we'd build and what it costs. If it makes sense, we start that week.
