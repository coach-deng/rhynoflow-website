FAQ

Common questions.

What kind of projects do you take on?

Web applications, business automation, websites, dashboards, APIs, and AI-powered tools. If it's software that helps your business run better, we probably build it. We specialise in Google Workspace automation and React web apps, but we're comfortable across the full stack.

How fast can you deliver?

Most projects go live in 3-7 days. A simple automation or landing page can be done in a day or two. A full web application with custom features takes about a week. We'll scope it on the first call so you know exactly what to expect.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. You tell us the problem. We figure out the technical solution. You'll get clear updates and see real progress — not jargon and ticket numbers.

What does setup look like?

We do a 30-minute call. You show us what's eating your time or what you need built. We scope it, give you a price, and start building. Most clients are live within a week of that first call.

How is this different from hiring an agency?

Agencies have account managers, project managers, and junior developers. You talk to five people and nothing ships for weeks. Here, you talk to the person writing the code. That's it. Faster, cheaper, and you know exactly who's building your stuff.

Who owns the code?

You do. Everything we build is yours — source code, hosting, data. If you stop working with us, nothing breaks and nothing disappears. Automations keep running in your Google account. Web apps stay on your hosting.

What if something breaks?

Monthly clients get ongoing support — we monitor and fix things as needed. Project clients get 30 days of support included. After that, we offer affordable retainers to keep things running smooth.

What if I want to cancel?

You cancel. No contracts, no exit fees. Everything stays yours and keeps working.

Let's talk about what you need built.

30 minutes, no pitch. You tell us the problem, we tell you what we'd build and what it costs. If it makes sense, we start that week.
