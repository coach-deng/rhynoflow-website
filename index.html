FAQ

Questions we always get.

What do I need to get started?

Gmail and Google Calendar. That's it. If you're not on Google Workspace yet, we'll help you get set up. It doesn't cost extra.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. We build everything. You just use your email like normal — except now half the work is already done before you open it.

What does setup actually look like?

We do a 30-minute call. You show us your week and what's eating your time. We go in, build it, test it, and walk you through before anything goes live. Most setups are done within a week.

How is this different from Zapier or Make?

Zapier and Make are tools you figure out yourself. You still need to set them up, fix them when they break, and pay another subscription. With Rhynoflow we build it and we maintain it. One fee. Nothing to manage.

Is my data safe?

Your data never leaves your Google account. The scripts run inside your own workspace under your login. We don't store anything. GDPR-friendly by design.

What if I want to cancel?

You cancel. No contracts, no hard feelings. Everything built stays in your Google account and keeps running. It's yours.

What if something breaks?

That's what the monthly fee is for. We keep an eye on everything. If Google updates something or a script hiccups, we fix it. You don't touch it.

How does payment work?

We invoice monthly. You pay via MobilePay or bank transfer. No subscriptions, no auto-charges. You get an invoice, you pay it. Simple.
