About Rhynoflow

Built out of necessity.
Built for people who actually work.

We're a small team of builders and specialists based in Copenhagen. We don't do templates, retainers-for-nothing, or software that nobody uses. We build things that run — and then we make sure they keep running.

The origin

A basketball coach needed software his club could actually use.

Deng Awak has been coaching youth basketball in Copenhagen for years. As the club grew, so did the admin — payment tracking, parent emails, game reminders, scheduling, funding deadlines. All of it falling on one person's shoulders.

He tried every app. Google Forms. Notion. Spreadsheets. None of it talked to each other. None of it ran itself.

So he built his own system — Google Apps Scripts running inside the club's Gmail and Calendar, handling everything automatically. Morning briefings. Payment alerts. Automated reminders. It worked so well that other clubs started asking about it.

That's Rhynoflow. Software built by someone who understood the problem from the inside — not a developer who'd never coached a day in their life.
