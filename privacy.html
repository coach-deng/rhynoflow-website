Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Rhynoflow is a one-person automation service based in Copenhagen, Denmark. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what your rights are. We keep it short because there's genuinely not much to say.

Who we are

Rhynoflow is operated by Deng Awak, Copenhagen, Denmark. Contact: hello@rhynoflow.com

What we collect

When you book a demo or get in touch:

  • Your name and email address
  • Any information you share during our call or in email

When you become a client:

  • Access to your Google Workspace account (granted by you, revokable at any time)
  • Business information needed to build your automations (contacts, calendar structure, email templates)

We do not collect payment card data directly. Invoices are handled via email and bank transfer.

How we use your data

We use your information only to:

  • Deliver and maintain the automation service you signed up for
  • Communicate with you about your setup, changes, and support
  • Invoice you for the service

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising. Ever.

Google Workspace access

Rhynoflow builds automations inside your Google account using Google Apps Script. This means:

  • Scripts run inside your account, not ours
  • We request the minimum permissions needed
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings
  • We never store copies of your emails or calendar data on our own servers

Data storage

Communication and client notes are stored in our own Google Workspace account. We do not use third-party CRM tools that store your data outside the EU.

Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with Datatilsynet (the Danish data protection authority)

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@rhynoflow.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies

This website uses no tracking cookies. The Cal.com booking embed may set its own cookies governed by Cal.com's privacy policy.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll change the date at the top. We'll notify active clients by email if the changes are significant.

Contact

Questions? Email hello@rhynoflow.com
